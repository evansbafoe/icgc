Welcome to ICGC

We are a vibrant and loving community under the Church of Pentecost. We are very delighted to have you join us today. Whether you are a long-time member or a first-time visitor, we want you to feel at home and be embraced by the love of Christ.
At ICGC, we are committed to growing in the faith, serving our community, and spreading the Good News of Jesus Christ. We believe that every individual is precious in the sight of God, and we are excited to embark on this spiritual journey together.
Join us for our dynamic Worship services, enriching Bible studies, and various Fellowship activities designed to build up your intimacy with God and not negleting fellowship with one another. As a church, we encourage active participation, through our God-giving talents and gifts share your talents and gifts.
May your time with us be filled with God's presence, joy, and blessings. We look forward to worshipping and growing in the Faith together and abounding in all goods works. In Christ's Love

Daily Devotion

As often as we do this, we build an intimacy with our heavenly father through the study of the word, prayer and worship and meditation.
